[9]_ .

Be taught, O brethren, be taught. Do not let us be without fear. Remember the things we read, 'It is a fearful thing to come into the hands of the living God' [34]_ ; and again, 'It is a fearful thing to expect the judgement and the fierceness [35]_ of the fire which will devour the adversaries' [36]_ .
